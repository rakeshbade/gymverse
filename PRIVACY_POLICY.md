# Privacy Policy for VyaM

**Effective Date:** January 4, 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to VyaM ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe user experience. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application VyaM (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

- **Email Address**: Used for authentication and account recovery
- **Name**: Used to personalize your experience
- **Physical Metrics**: 
  - Age
  - Gender
  - Weight (kg)
  - Height (cm)
  - Fitness Goal
  - Fitness Level

### Workout Data

We collect and store:

- **Workout History**: Records of completed workouts
- **Favorites**: Workouts you've marked as favorites
- **Activity Data**: Duration, calories burned, and exercises completed

### Automatically Collected Information

We do not collect any device information, analytics, or tracking data beyond what's necessary for app functionality.

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

1. **Provide Services**: Enable authentication and personalized workout experiences
2. **Calculate Metrics**: Compute calories burned using your physical metrics and the MET formula
3. **Store Preferences**: Sync your favorites and workout history across devices
4. **Improve Experience**: Customize workout recommendations based on your fitness level

## Data Storage and Security

- **Storage**: All personal data is securely stored using Google Firebase
- **Encryption**: Data is transmitted using industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS)
- **Access Control**: You can only access your own data; other users cannot view your information
- **No Third-Party Access**: We never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Delete**: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
- **Update**: Modify your profile information at any time through the app
- **Export**: Request an export of your workout history

To exercise these rights, please contact us at [your-email@example.com].

## Children's Privacy

VyaM requires users to be at least 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

## Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we will delete all associated personal data within 30 days.
